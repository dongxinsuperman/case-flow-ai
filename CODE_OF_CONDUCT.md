# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project should be a practical, respectful engineering space. Participants
are expected to:

- Be direct and constructive.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence and context.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.
- Respect privacy and do not publish credentials, private endpoints, or customer data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- Insults, threats, or sustained personal attacks.
- Sexualized, discriminatory, or harassing language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Intentionally submitting secrets or private company data.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, or block
participants when behavior harms the project or other contributors.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers privately through
the repository owner's available GitHub contact path.
